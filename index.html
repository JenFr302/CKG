Introduction

Core Keeper is a sandbox adventure game that combines mining, crafting, exploration, and survival. Players awaken deep underground, drawn toward a mysterious ancient Core that pulses with energy. The goal? Uncover its secrets while building, farming, and fighting through procedurally generated caverns.

Whether you're playing solo or with friends, the game offers dynamic environments, powerful boss battles,and endless opportunities for creativity and resource gathering.

Trailer

Watch the official launch trailer to get a glimpse of the vast underground world, exciting combat, and creative base-building possibilities that lie ahead.

Gameplay

Gameplay in Core Keeper revolves around gathering resources, improving equipment, and rebuilding a thriving base near the ancient Core. Players can mine rare ores, cultivate crops for sustenance, and craft intricate setups for automation.

Each area is filled with danger and mystery—creatures lurk in the dark, and biomes shift in both tone and reward as you explore deeper.

Exploration

The underground world of Core Keeper is procedurally generated, ensuring a unique experience every time you play. From glowing crystal caverns to fungal jungles, the world is filled with treasures and secrets waiting to be discovered.

Players can chart their own path, delve into ancient ruins, and unlock new technologies by uncovering lost relics.

Community

Core Keeper features a growing community of players who share strategies, fan creations, and updates. The game continues to evolve, with frequent updates and developer interactions adding new content and expanding gameplay depth.

Join the discussions, share your base designs, and connect with other explorers in the official forums or Discord server.
